## One of my favorite horror movie is Shutter.
1. Shutter is a horror movie about a photographer and his girlfriend who are haunted by the ghost of a young woman after a hit and run car accident they caused. The ghost's image begins appearing in the photographer's developed photographs and the couple is soon discovers the haunting is tied to a shocking secret from their past involving the victim and the photographer's friends.
2. The film is known for it's eerie ghost imagery and a twist ending that reveals the extent of the photographer's involvement in the events leading to the ghost's death.
3. The movie was released on the year of 2004.
4. Shutter from 2004 has also the same plot from the remake Shutter in 2008.
